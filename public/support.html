About

Support MediPrimer

MediPrimer is free, carries no ads, and sells nothing. That independence is the whole point — and it's also what we protect most carefully when it comes to funding.

A resource like this only works if you can trust that no one is paying to influence what it says. So we're clear and public about how it can be supported.

Ways to support this resource

  • Grants. Funding from foundations focused on health literacy, aging, or community health — grants that support the mission without any stake in individual coverage decisions.
  • Sponsorship / underwriting, done right. Public-broadcasting style: a clearly disclosed, non-conflicted supporter's name, with zero editorial control over any content.
  • Spread the word. The most valuable help costs nothing: if MediPrimer helped you, tell a friend who's turning 65, or ask your local senior center, library, or SHIP to link to it.

Our editorial-independence promise

Any support we accept comes with one non-negotiable condition: no supporter, donor, or grantor has any say over what we write. Content is based solely on official government sources and our commitment to plain, neutral, accurate information. If a supporter ever asked us to soften, slant, or steer our content, we would decline the support. Supporters are disclosed; influence is not for sale. See our Editorial Standards.

We don't collect personal information or sell your data. We use Google Analytics only to see which pages help people.

Are you a foundation, nonprofit, library, or community organization interested in supporting or partnering with MediPrimer through a grant or clearly-disclosed underwriting? We welcome inquiries that fit the standards above at editor@mediprimer.org.

Thank you for helping keep independent, plain-language health-coverage information free for the people who need it.
